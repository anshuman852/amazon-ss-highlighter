Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 23, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Amazon Subscribe & Save Highlighter Chrome extension ("we", "our", or "extension") handles your information when you use our extension.

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information or user data. The Amazon Subscribe & Save Highlighter extension operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any data to external servers.

How We Use Information

The extension only processes content on Amazon India (amazon.in) pages locally in your browser to:

  • Identify products eligible for Subscribe & Save
  • Apply visual highlighting to eligible products
  • Identify and optionally dim or hide sponsored products
  • Display a badge with the count of Subscribe & Save products

Data Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage API to save only your preferences:

  • Highlight color
  • Non-Subscribe & Save product display mode (normal/dim/hide)
  • Sponsored product display mode (normal/dim/hide)
  • Whether the extension is enabled

These preferences are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted elsewhere.

Third-Party Access

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information.

Host Permissions

The extension requires access to amazon.in to identify and highlight Subscribe & Save eligible products. It does not access or interact with any other websites.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension accordingly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email address provided in the Chrome Web Store listing.
